FAQ
Got questions?
Do I need marketing experience to use BrandFlow?
+
Not at all. BrandFlow is designed for business owners, not marketers. If you can answer a few questions about your business, BrandFlow does everything else — writing, formatting, hashtags, and CTAs included.
How does BrandFlow learn my brand voice?
+
During onboarding you answer 5 questions about your business — what you do, who your customers are, your tone of voice, and what makes you different. BrandFlow uses this to build your Brand DNA, which it references every time it generates content for you.
Is there a free trial?
+
We don't offer a free trial, but every plan comes with a 14-day money-back guarantee — no questions asked. If you're not happy for any reason within the first 14 days, we'll refund you in full.
What platforms does BrandFlow generate content for?
+
BrandFlow generates content for Facebook Ads, Instagram, Email campaigns, LinkedIn, Twitter/X, and TikTok. The Growth and Agency plans include all six platforms. The Starter plan covers Facebook, Instagram, and Email.
Can I cancel at any time?
+
Absolutely. There are no contracts and no lock-ins. You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings, and you won't be charged again from your next billing cycle.
Is my business information private and secure?
+
Yes. Your brand data is never shared, sold, or used to train AI models. It's stored securely and only used to generate your own content. We take privacy seriously.
